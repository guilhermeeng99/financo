Your finances, organized effortlessly

Financo is a personal finance manager with an AI assistant built into the app. Log expenses by text, photo, or voice — the AI turns them into transactions you confirm with a tap. Free and open source, in the browser and on Android.

Everything you need to track your money

From daily logging to monthly planning — accounts, cards, transfers, budgets, and reports in one place.

📊

Smart dashboard

Filter by month or year, income-vs-expense summary, category breakdown, and a monthly evolution chart.

🏦

Accounts & cards

Checking accounts and credit cards. Cards link to a paying account, with closing day, due day, and available credit.

🔁

Transactions & transfers

Standalone entries or transfers between your own accounts, with a running balance per account. Single, installment, or fixed recurrences.

📅

Payables & receivables

Future-dated entries stay pending, become overdue within their month, and can be marked as paid or received.

🎯

50/30/20 budget

Classify categories into needs, wants, and savings and track your targets through the month.

📁

CSV import

Bulk-create accounts, categories, transactions, and budgets from CSV files, with ready-made templates included.

AI assistant

An AI that understands your finances

Financo's chat uses Gemini (Vertex AI) through Cloud Functions. Describe an expense, send a photo of a receipt or a notification, or speak it out loud — the model proposes structured transactions, transfers, accounts, and categories. You confirm them on an action card; nothing is saved without your confirmation.

⌨️

Text

"Lunch, 38 on the card" becomes a ready-made transaction.

📷

Image

Receipts, notification screenshots, and invoices become entries.

🎙️

Voice

Say the expense and the AI transcribes and structures it for you.
